Strawberries and Cream
Breakfast smoothie

Ingrediets

1 Banana
1 Handful of Strawberries
1 TBSP Chia Seeds
1/2 Scoop of Vanilla Protein powdeer
100ml Almond Milk (more or less depending on your preference)
125ml Greek Yogurt

Method

Throw all ingredients into a highspeed blender (preferably place the chia seeds in last)
and blend until smooth. Pour in a pretty glass with a straw. Serve immediately.
